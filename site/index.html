Repository contracts, not repo chaos.

Validate structure, enforce templates and frontmatter schemas, and automate safe cleanup with full logs.

Quickstart

pnpm --filter @supernal/repotype build
pnpm --filter @supernal/repotype exec repotype validate .
pnpm --filter @supernal/repotype exec repotype status . --json
pnpm --filter @supernal/repotype exec repotype apply .

Safe Cleanup Flow

repotype cleanup-run . --queue sort_queue --min-errors 3 --dry-run
repotype cleanup-run . --queue sort_queue --min-errors 3
# logs:
# sort_queue/cleanup-log.jsonl
# sort_queue/cleanup-log.md
